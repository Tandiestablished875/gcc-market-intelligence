# Soft-Landing Programs for Foreign Founders — GCC

> Last verified: May 2026. Eligibility, ticket sizes, and program intake change frequently. Verify on the official program site before committing capital or relocating.

## How to use this file

Soft-landing programs are the single highest-leverage entry path for foreign B2B/B2G founders into the GCC. The right program gives you: legal entity setup, residency visas, office space, sometimes cash and equity-free incentives, plus warm intros to potential customers and co-investors.

Programs are organized below by **country**, then by **stage / focus**. The "Best for" line tells you which founder profile fits each program. Read your target country's file (`references/countries/`) alongside this.

## Saudi Arabia

### NTDP (National Technology Development Program)

- **Body:** MCIT.
- **Best for:** deep-tech / tech founders with payroll in KSA.
- **Headline benefit:** **Tech Crew — 50% salary subsidy** for up to 18 months for qualifying technical hires (Saudi or international). Multiple other tracks (Tech Mentorship, Tech Visa, etc.).
- **Stage:** seed–Series B.
- **Verify:** https://ntdp.gov.sa.

### Garage (by The Garage)

- **Body:** MCIT + STC + KAUST + private partners. Operates Riyadh and other locations.
- **Best for:** early-stage / MVP-stage tech founders building inside the Saudi ecosystem.
- **Headline benefit:** accelerator + co-working + investor network. ~6-month programs.
- **Stage:** pre-seed–seed.
- **Verify:** https://www.thegarage.sa.

### Misk Innovation

- **Body:** MiSK Foundation (MBS's philanthropy).
- **Best for:** young Saudi-aligned tech and creative founders. Some foreign founders accepted with strong KSA alignment.
- **Programs:** Tomoh accelerator, Misk Apex, Misk Launchpad, Misk fellowships.
- **Verify:** https://misk.org.sa.

### Sanabil 500 Global

- **Body:** Sanabil Investments (PIF) + 500 Global.
- **Best for:** early-stage tech with regional ambitions.
- **Headline benefit:** **$100k SAFE** + 12-week accelerator in Riyadh.
- **Stage:** pre-seed.
- **Verify:** https://sanabil.500.global / via Sanabil's site.

### Wa'ed Ventures

- **Body:** Aramco.
- **Best for:** energy / advanced tech founders with relevance to Aramco supply chain or downstream.
- **Headline benefit:** $250k–$5M tickets; access to Aramco Garage incubation; pilots with Aramco entities.
- **Stage:** seed–Series A.
- **Verify:** https://www.waed.net.

### Aramco Garage

- **Body:** Saudi Aramco.
- **Best for:** energy / industrial deep-tech.
- **Headline benefit:** pilot pathway to Aramco contracts; incubation in Dhahran.
- **Verify:** https://aramcogarage.com.

### KAUST / The Garage at KAUST / TAQADAM

- **Body:** King Abdullah University of Science and Technology.
- **Best for:** deep-tech / hard science / research-backed startups.
- **Headline benefit:** 6-month TAQADAM accelerator; ~$10B KAUST endowment supports research commercialization; KAUST campus is one of the most physical-infrastructure-rich research zones in the region.
- **Verify:** https://www.kaust.edu.sa.

### NEOM Investment Office

- **Status:** NEOM is under proposed restructuring as of Jan 2026 (not finalized).
- **Best for:** deep-tech / mobility / energy / biotech with $5M+ Phase 1 fit.
- **Caveat:** verify current organization with MISA before pitching.

### Madinah Tech Cultivator

- **Body:** Madinah Region Development Authority.
- **Best for:** deep-tech / smart-city / sustainability / mobility / energy / food security founders pitching municipal customers.
- **Headline benefit:** dedicated pilot pathway into Madinah Municipality + adjacent smart-city procurement. Cohort 1 (2024–25) had 20 startups from 11 countries; 80% deep tech; 75% with paying users.
- **Anchor reference:** the founder profile this entire skill was designed around.
- **Verify:** https://madinahcultivator.com.

### MISA / Invest Saudi

- **Best for:** all foreign founders seeking the foundational licensing.
- **Headline benefit:** **Investment Registration Certificate (IRC)** — replaced the old SAGIA/MISA license regime under the February 2025 Investment Law. Days, not months. 100% foreign ownership in most sectors. SAR 500k common minimum capital; SAR 30M+ for fully foreign trading/retail.
- **Verify:** https://misa.gov.sa.

### Monsha'at — SME General Authority

- **Best for:** SME-stage founders with Saudi operations.
- **Headline benefit:** SME finance programs, Biban conference, Kafalah loan guarantees.
- **Verify:** https://www.monshaat.gov.sa.

### SAMA Fintech Sandbox

- **Best for:** fintech founders.
- **Headline benefit:** controlled testing environment with regulatory exemptions for up to 24 months.
- **Verify:** https://www.sama.gov.sa.

### CMA Fintech Lab

- **Best for:** capital-markets / securities-tech / robo-advisory fintech founders.
- **Verify:** https://cma.org.sa.

### Special Economic Zones (post-2023 SEZ Law)

- KAEC SEZ, Jazan SEZ, Ras Al Khair SEZ, Cloud Computing SEZ (virtual), SILZ (Riyadh airport-adjacent).
- **Best for:** specific sector and geographic fits.

## UAE

### Hub71 (Abu Dhabi)

- **Body:** Mubadala + Government of Abu Dhabi.
- **Best for:** tech founders aligned with Abu Dhabi priorities — AI, ClimateTech, Web3/Digital Assets, Life Sciences, mobility, ECA Anjal Z.
- **Headline benefit:** Company Building Program — **AED 250k in-kind + AED 250k cash via SAFE + AED 250k high-performance grant = up to AED 750k total.** ~200+ alumni have raised ~AED 5B+.
- **Verticals:**
  - **Hub71+ AI** (general AI track).
  - **Hub71+ ClimateTech** (Plug and Play partnership).
  - **Hub71+ Digital Assets** (Elixir Capital partnership).
  - **Hub71+ Life Sciences**.
  - **SAVI** (mobility).
  - **ECA Anjal Z** (advanced tech youth track).
- **Stage:** seed–Series B.
- **Verify:** https://hub71.com.

### DIFC Innovation Hub + FinTech Hive

- **Best for:** fintech, regtech, insurtech, Islamic finance founders.
- **Headline benefit:** 12-week FinTech Hive accelerator; 200+ alumni. DIFC license (English common law jurisdiction); fintech-friendly regulation.
- **Verify:** https://www.difc.ae.

### ADGM RegLab

- **Body:** Abu Dhabi Global Market.
- **Best for:** fintech, virtual assets, sustainable finance, RegTech founders.
- **Headline benefit:** Independent regulatory sandbox (one of the first in the region). 2-year operation. English common law.
- **Verify:** https://www.adgm.com.

### in5 (TECOM, Dubai)

- **Best for:** tech / media / design / science founders.
- **Headline benefit:** 500+ alumni; alumni raised AED 8B+. Subsidized office, mentorship, investor network. Multiple campuses (in5 Tech, in5 Media, in5 Design, in5 Science, in5 Gaming).
- **Verify:** https://in5.ae.

### Sheraa (Sharjah Entrepreneurship Center)

- **Founder/CEO:** Sheikha Bodour bint Sultan Al Qasimi.
- **Best for:** early-stage founders; particularly strong on female-founded ventures (51%+ historically).
- **Headline benefit:** 4 Centers of Excellence; 450+ startups; **Access Sharjah Challenge with up to $136k prizes**. Free residence visa support.
- **Verify:** https://sheraa.ae.

### DMCC (Dubai Multi Commodities Centre)

- **Best for:** commodities, trading, crypto, gaming, AI, Web3 founders.
- **Headline benefit:** large free zone; competitive licensing; DMCC Crypto Centre; DMCC Gaming Centre; DMCC AI Centre.
- **Verify:** https://www.dmcc.ae.

### Dubai Future Foundation (DFF) / Area 2071 / Dubai Future Accelerators

- **Best for:** deep-tech founders with government-relevant solutions.
- **Headline benefit:** access to Dubai government as pilot partner; sponsored cohort programs.
- **Verify:** https://www.dubaifuture.ae.

### MBR Innovation Fund

- **Best for:** UAE-aligned innovation projects.
- **Verify:** Dubai government innovation portal.

### Khalifa Fund

- **Best for:** Emirati-founder-led ventures primarily; some open programs.
- **Verify:** https://www.khalifafund.ae.

### RAKEZ (Ras Al Khaimah Economic Zone)

- **Best for:** cost-minimization free-zone licensing.
- **Headline benefit:** licenses from AED 7,500 — among the cheapest UAE free-zone setup options.
- **Verify:** https://rakez.com.

### KEZAD (Khalifa Economic Zones Abu Dhabi)

- **Best for:** industrial / manufacturing / logistics founders.
- **Verify:** https://www.kezad.ae.

### Masdar City Free Zone (Abu Dhabi)

- **Best for:** clean-tech / renewable energy founders.
- **Verify:** https://masdarcityfreezone.ae.

### twofour54 (Abu Dhabi)

- **Best for:** media / content / production founders.
- **Verify:** https://www.twofour54.com.

### Golden Visa (federal-level UAE)

- 10-year residence visa for: investors (≥AED 2M property OR ≥AED 2M business investment), entrepreneurs, specialized talent (PhDs, doctors, scientists, engineers in priority areas), exceptional students.

## Qatar

### Startup Qatar Investment Program (QDB)

- **Body:** Qatar Development Bank.
- **Best for:** early to growth-stage tech founders soft-landing in Qatar.
- **Headline benefit:** **up to $500k early-stage; up to $5M growth-stage.** Also a fast-track corporate license at Web Summit Qatar each year. Feb 2025 Pavilion: 1,634 companies registered, 156 licenses issued same day.
- **Verify:** https://startupqatar.qa.

### QSTP (Qatar Science & Technology Park)

- **Body:** Qatar Foundation.
- **Best for:** deep-tech / R&D-collaboration founders.
- **Headline benefit:** access to Qatar Foundation universities (Carnegie Mellon, Georgetown, Northwestern, Texas A&M, HEC Paris, UCL — at Education City); ProductDev grants; free-zone status.
- **Verify:** https://qstp.org.qa.

### QFC (Qatar Financial Centre)

- **Best for:** financial services, services, fintech, holding-company founders.
- **Headline benefit:** **10% CIT on local-source profits only;** 100% foreign ownership; English common law; no FX controls.
- **Verify:** https://www.qfc.qa.

### Qatar Central Bank Fintech Regulatory Sandbox

- **Best for:** fintech founders.
- **Verify:** https://www.qcb.gov.qa.

### Digital Incubation Center (DIC) / IdeaCamp

- **Best for:** early-stage Qatari and resident founders.
- **Headline benefit:** up to QAR 100k (~$27k) seed grant.

### Qatar FinTech Hub (QFTH)

- **Body:** QDB.
- **Verify:** https://www.qfth.qa.

## Bahrain

### EDB Fast-Track (Investor Concierge)

- **Body:** Bahrain Economic Development Board (chaired by Crown Prince/PM Sheikh Salman bin Hamad).
- **Best for:** **all** foreign B2B/B2G founders entering Bahrain — single best entry path.
- **Headline benefit:** **FREE concierge service** — pre-arrival market briefing, business registration (3–5 day CR setup), residency/visa coordination, customer intros, grant program access, office space connections.
- **Verify:** https://bahrainedb.com/thinkbahrain.

### CBB Regulatory Sandbox

- **Body:** Central Bank of Bahrain.
- **Best for:** fintech, crypto, payments, RegTech, InsurTech founders.
- **Headline benefit:** since June 2017. **12 months operation; BHD 100 fee; 15-day review; rolling intake; open to foreign + joint applications.**
- **Verify:** https://www.cbb.gov.bh/regulatory-sandbox/.

### FinHub 973 (CBB + Fintech Galaxy)

- Joint platform supporting fintech ecosystem.
- **Verify:** https://www.finhub973.com.

### Bahrain FinTech Bay (BFB)

- 10,000 sq ft fintech hub in Manama.
- **Verify:** https://www.bahrainfintechbay.com.

### Tamkeen (Labour Fund)

- **Established 2006.** BHD 830M cumulative private-sector injection; supported 41,420 Bahrainis + 8,700 enterprises in 2024 alone.
- **Foreign-founder gotcha:** most Tamkeen grants require a Bahraini employee on payroll. Solve by incorporating a Bahrain CR + hiring ≥1 Bahraini.
- **Programs:**
  - **Business Development Program** — 50% co-match.
  - **5K Support** — BHD 5,000 cap.
  - **Start Your Business** — incubation.
  - **National Employment Program 3.0** — wage subsidies for hiring Bahrainis.
  - **Raise Program** (Spring Venture Services + Salica) — direct startup funding.
  - **Overseas Expansion** — supports Bahrain companies expanding to KSA/GCC.
- **Verify:** https://www.tamkeen.bh.

### NBB Innovation Program

- **Body:** National Bank of Bahrain.
- **Demo Day:** Feb 4, 2026.
- **Headline benefit:** $10k cash + 3-month incubation + NBB pilot opportunity.

### Standard Chartered Women in Tech Bahrain

- **Headline benefit:** $35k / $25k / $15k prizes for 1st / 2nd / 3rd + 3-month residency.

### BDB Private Credit SME Growth Fund

- $265M launched 2024. Bahrain Development Bank.

## Kuwait

### KDIPA Direct Investment License

- **Body:** Kuwait Direct Investment Promotion Authority.
- **Best for:** all foreign B2B/B2G founders entering Kuwait — required path.
- **Headline benefit:** 100% foreign ownership; **up to 10 years CIT exemption (renewable for another 10);** customs/import duty relief; land allocation.
- **Scoring (Resolution 329/2019):** tech transfer + Kuwaiti job creation + market dev + sustainability. <30% = rejection; 5/15 = license only; 13/15 = full incentives.
- **Recent activity:** FY 2024–25 approved KD 222.9M (~$725M) new FDI; cumulative since 2015 = KD 1.97B / 105 entities / 34 countries.
- **Verify:** https://www.kdipa.gov.kw.

### KNF (Kuwait National Fund for SME Development)

- **Status:** KWD 2B (~$6.5B) fund.
- **Eligibility:** restricted to Kuwaiti citizens / Kuwaiti-led companies. **Foreign founders effectively excluded.**

### KFAS (Kuwait Foundation for the Advancement of Sciences)

- Research and innovation grant body. Foreign deep-tech founders with Kuwait university collaboration can engage.

### Central Bank of Kuwait Regulatory Sandbox

- For fintech / regtech.

## Oman

### OPAZ Free Zone licensing (post-RD 38/2025, April 2025)

- **Best for:** all foreign B2B/B2G founders — **the cheapest GCC market entry post-April 2025.**
- **Headline benefit:** 10-year CIT exemption (renewable 2 x 10 yrs = **up to 30 years**); 100% foreign ownership; no minimum capital; 50-year land lease (renewable); duty-free import; free profit repatriation.
- **Free zones to choose from:**
  - **SEZAD (Duqm)** — at 2,000 km², the largest free zone in MENA. Heavy industry, energy, logistics, space.
  - **Sohar Free Zone** — petrochemicals, steel.
  - **Salalah Free Zone** — manufacturing.
  - **Knowledge Oasis Muscat (KOM)** — ICT and services.
  - **Al Mazunah** — Yemen border-trade.
- **Verify:** https://opaz.gov.om.

### Future Fund Oman (FFO)

- **Body:** OIA.
- **Best for:** growth-stage projects with substantial capital ask.
- **Headline benefit:** OMR 2B (~$5.2B) over 5 years (2024–28). Direct project max OMR 100M each, ≤40% equity. Co-investment funds: IDG Oman Fund ($200M w/ IDG Capital); EWTP Oman Fund ($250M w/ EW Partners). 12% target return.
- **Recent activity:** 141 projects approved in 2025; $1.2B committed; $2.1B private capital mobilized; 1,400+ jobs.

### Phaze Ventures

- Oman's first private VC; $30M fund (late 2021). $100k–$500k seed / Series A tickets. Strong B2B / B2G / deep-tech preference.

### Oman Technology Fund (OTF)

- $200M fund (est. 2016). Regional VC.

### Invest Oman fast-tracking

- License within 1 day of fee payment for many activities.
- **Verify:** https://gov.om/en (Invest Oman section).

## How to choose

If you are a **deep-tech founder** with a B2B + B2G motion and $50k–$250k existing funding (the anchor profile this skill was built for, per Madinah Tech Cultivator Cohort 1):

1. **First port of call:** the soft-landing program of your target country (NTDP, Hub71, Sheraa, Startup Qatar, EDB Fast-Track, KDIPA, OPAZ).
2. **Sector overlay:** SAMA / DIFC / ADGM / CBB / QCB sandboxes if you're fintech; ADGM RegLab also for sustainable finance and RegTech.
3. **City overlay:** Madinah Tech Cultivator if you fit the smart-city / sustainability / mobility / energy / food security profile.
4. **Capital overlay:** if you're already at Series A with a $5M+ round forming, Hub71 + Sanabil 500 + Wa'ed + QDB Startup Qatar + Mubadala / MGX / Alat are the strategic-capital coverage.

## What outside founders should know

- Programs publish "what you get" loudly. The quieter benefit is **warm intros and credibility laundering** — being a "Hub71 cohort company" or "NTDP awardee" opens enterprise customer doors that cold outreach never will.
- **Don't apply to every program.** Pick 2–3 max. Multiple parallel applications dilute attention and signal weak commitment.
- **Programs have intake cycles.** Plan around them. Most run 1–2 cohorts per year.
- **Most programs do not have visa fast-tracking** for you immediately. You'll still need a separate residency or employment visa unless the program explicitly states otherwise.

## What NOT to do

- Don't accept program equity terms without legal review — some programs take warrants or have material commitment requirements.
- Don't relocate based on a program letter that doesn't yet have a signed offer.
- Don't assume Tamkeen grants without a Bahraini employee. The filter is hard.
- Don't apply to KNF as a foreign founder — you're not eligible.

## Verify before committing capital

- Most program details change quarterly. Verify all financial terms (ticket sizes, equity, runway) on the official program website immediately before applying.
- For tax incentives (RHQ, OPAZ, KDIPA, QFC), get a regional tax adviser to confirm your eligibility before relocating.
